⚡ STAND-OFF ⚡

🤠 HIGH NOON SHOWDOWN 🤠

🔫 Face off in the ultimate duel where quick thinking beats quick draws! Players circle up for a battle of wits, strategy, and lightning-fast reflexes. 💀 Only the smartest gunslinger survives!

🎯 How to Play

Players stand in a circle
Each player has two imaginary guns
One action per round
Each gun holds only one bullet

⚔️ CHOOSE YOUR ACTION ⚔️

🔫

SHOOT

Point both guns at your target! Make your shot count - you only get one bullet per gun!

💥
⬆️

RELOAD

Point guns skyward to reload! Essential for survival - can't shoot without bullets!

🔄
🛡️

BLOCK

Cross arms in X-shape! Your shield against incoming fire - timing is everything!

✨

🎲 Round Sequence 🎲

1️⃣
Count Together
"One, Two..."
🎭
Declare & Act
Say action + gesture
⚖️
Resolve
Who got shot?
🔄
Repeat
Until one remains

🧠 Strategic Tips 🧠

🎯
Track the Ammo
Remember who's loaded and who needs to reload!
🤔
Bluff & Misdirect
Make opponents think you're targeting them!
⏰
Perfect Timing
Know when to block and when to strike!
👥
Alliance & Betrayal
Temporary truces can lead to victory!
👑

LAST GUNSLINGER STANDING WINS!

The sole survivor claims the title of fastest mind in the west! 🏆
