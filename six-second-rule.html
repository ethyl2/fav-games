How to play: Determine a playing order -- who will be first, second, etc. One person is the judge per round. The judge clicks 'get category' and reads the resulting cateogory aloud! Then the judge starts the timer. The person to the judge's left is in the hot seat and has to give three answers under 6 seconds. If the person can't, no points are given. However, if the person gives three answers, the judge starts the timer again and the next person tries to give three different answers from the first person. This continues until someone makes a mistakes or has a brain fart and is stomped! When that happens, the point is given to the LAST person who gave the last three different answers from anyone else. Then that person becomes the next judge. The game continues until someone gets 6 points.
