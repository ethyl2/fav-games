How to Create a Guess That Song Game

Here's how to create a fun song-guessing game, inspired by the podcast Good Job Brain.

You can create audio files made of snippets of lyrics, spoken by unusual voices.

Then, you can play them for your friends and see if they can guess the song.

3 Easy Steps

  1. Pick a song and choose lyrics that don't immediately give the song away.
  2. Use a text-to-voice generator, like voicegenerator.io/ to turn those lyrics into an audio file.
  3. If you'd like, convert the audio file's voice to something more unusual, using a tool such as voicechanger.io/.
